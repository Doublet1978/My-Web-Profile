Learning Experience

Thanks to Chris Raffety who ensures each topic in the modules were delivered in its simplest form and ensuring a easy understanding of the web design and development course, like the introduction of the Hypertext mark-up language (HTML) as the root to web development , Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) as the controller, which helps to style and format the html, its content, layout, colors, and fonts. Semantic Tags, Git and Git Hub, the debugger from the inspect link, as it enables me to inspect and modify the HTML and CSS in real time. As sufficient materials were made available from week one , there was little or no need to search for material outside the provided slides to develop the Website.

Development and design

A sketch drawing with a five pages layout i.e., Home, Contact, Project, Video Demo and Site Report, using the brief to outline the requirements was the first step. I took inspiration from Mark Junior and John Kendric's existing portfolios designs on social media(YouTube), a dark background with cool green and white combination was choosen in contrast with the glowish blue and white used by both of them, and a Mark Script Regular font from google fonts, this font I discovered is rarely used. A 5 HTML (index.html, contact.html, project.html, videoDem.html, site repot.html) and 3 CSS (main.css, desktop.css, and mobile.css) files were created to develop a responsive website.

Constraints

As a family person with three boys of 16, 14, and 10 years old and a full time job, the major constraints I had was balancing the study with parenthood and work conditioned starting this assignment late into the fifth week. The final problemm encountered was in the recording, too much noise from the laptop or breaks in audio, I must have had about 10 trials including.

Reflections

I could have developed a more completed and better design if I had approached the assignment on weekly bases, at least from week two. Doing the exercises in the slides before attempting to start the AS1 assignment, and the understanding of the developer's tool has really helped improve my understanding of the module and assist in the delivery of the assignment on time. Moreso, I have to developed my web without a Java Script as against sample design.
